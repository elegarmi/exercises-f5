Sponge cake

Ingredients

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 170°C for 10 min
  • Beat eggs
  • Melt butter
  • Add sugar and sifted flour
  • Mix until the dough is homogeneous, smooth and without lumps
  • Grease mold with butter and breadcrumbs
  • Insert the cake for 3 quarters of an hour to an hour with heat from below (look at 3/4 if the toothpick comes out clean)

Time

Aprox 1 hour
